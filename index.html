Who we are

Driving lessons built around you

At ISM Driving Solutions, we help learners across the Wirral and surrounding areas become confident, skilled, and safe drivers. We believe that learning to drive should feel supportive, not stressful.

Whether you're sitting behind the wheel for the very first time or fine-tuning your skills before your test, every lesson is tailored to your pace and your personal learning style. With a strong first-time pass rate and extensive experience with nervous learners, we make sure every student feels motivated and ready.

What we offer

Our services

From your very first lesson to passing your test and beyond — we have everything covered.

🚀

Beginner courses

Never been behind the wheel? We start from the very basics and build your skills gradually in a calm, supportive environment.

🎯

Test preparation

Targeted lessons covering the skills and manoeuvres you'll need on test day — so you walk in feeling fully prepared.

📄

Mock exams

Realistic test simulations that replicate the real experience — eliminating nerves and surprises before the big day.

🔄

Refresher lessons

Been off the road for a while? Refresher lessons help you rebuild confidence and update your skills quickly.

🛣

Motorway lessons

Post-test tuition for newly qualified drivers wanting to build confidence on faster roads safely and properly.

🛡

Defensive driving

Advanced skills to make you a safer, more aware driver — going beyond simply passing the test.

🔍

Assessment sessions

Not sure where to start? An assessment session helps us understand your level and plan the best route forward.
